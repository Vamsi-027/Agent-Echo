# Voice Profile

This profile outlines the tone, voice, and formatting style for posts.

## Core Tone
- **Direct & First-Person**: Write from the perspective of an active developer/builder ("I built...", "We ran into...").
- **No Jargon/Fluff**: Avoid words like "supercharge", "revolutionize", "excited to announce", "humbled and honored". 
- **Authentic & Pragmatic**: Share details, metrics, and raw findings. Focus on why something broke or what was learned rather than self-promotion.

## Format Guidelines
- **Opening**: Start with a hook that gets straight to the point (no generic greetings or rhetorical questions).
- **Structure**: Break text into short, scannable paragraphs (2-3 sentences max). Use bullet points for steps or data.
- **Emoji Usage**: Use emojis sparingly (maximum 2-3 per post), only to bullet-point or highlight. Never at the end of every line.
- **Closing**: End with a concrete takeaway, lesson, or a genuine, open-ended question that naturally invites peer discussion (no generic engagement prompts).
